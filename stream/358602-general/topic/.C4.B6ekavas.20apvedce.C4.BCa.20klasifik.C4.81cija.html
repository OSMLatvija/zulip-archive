HellMap (Jan 05 2025 at 11:27):

highway=motorway norāda (akcents mans):

Selected limited-access roads which can be classified as motorways include those that:

  • generally resemble a controlled-access road and are free from intersection controls (such as traffic-signals, rotaries and roundabouts, and stop signs or stop markings);
  • have at least two lanes in each traffic direction with some degree of separation;
  • have access roads only for [..] - tādu nav

Ņemot vērā "depending on the local context and prevailing convention" un "subject to local tagging conventions", manuprāt Ķekavas apvedceļa posmi atbilst tam. Un motorroad=* tā arī min - "that are not a motorway" - ja augstākminētās prasības neatbilstu motorway vai arī vietējā kopiena (ne)būtu nolēmusi likt motorway.

To ceļu uz šo brīdi ir rediģējuši daudzi kartētāji un, cik zinu, neviens nav iebildis (vai arī nav aizdomājies) par motorroad vs motorway. Bet speciāli arī apriests arī nav, cik zinu.
