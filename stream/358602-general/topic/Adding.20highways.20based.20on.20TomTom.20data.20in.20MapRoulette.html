(Below I’m including a Latvian translation of this message, created using DeepL.)

First of all, a big thank you for your contributions in our open MapRoulette challenges for fixing inconsistent building and highway intersections, and correcting inconsistent bridge and tunnel tagging. We really appreciate your help!

My team at TomTom now identified a number of highways in Latvia that are in our TomTom map but not yet in OpenStreetMap. After manually verifying the presence of these ways on the available imagery sources, we created a new MapRoulette challenge to help add them to OSM. The challenge contains 31 tasks and you can find it here: Latvia - Add Highways.

Our editing team will take up this challenge in a couple of weeks or so, and of course other community members are very welcome to try it out as well. If a way shouldn’t be added to the map, you can mark the task as “Not an issue”.

Don’t hesitate to let me know if you have any comments or questions.

Have a great week!

Marjan
