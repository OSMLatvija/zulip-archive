Topic: New Maproulette: Latvia - Nodes Without Primary Tags

Priscilla Zachee (Feb 24 2026 at 14:32):

Hello OSM Latvia,

A new MapRoulette challenge is live:  Latvia - Nodes Without Primary Tags!
The aim of this challenge is to improve data completeness by adding appropriate primary tags to OSM nodes that currently have a name=* tag but lack top-level tags defining the features (such as amenity=, shop=, place=*, etc.)

Your comments, feedback, and suggestions are very welcome.

Thank you,

Priscilla - TomTom

HellMap (Feb 24 2026 at 14:54):

Hi, looks good!

I would advise anyone slight caution when editing these, some need special care to fix, including research and surveys. Some don't have any OSM established tags to use.

There is also a daily report at https://osmlatvija.github.io/Osmalyzer/Non-defining%20Tagging%20report.html that detects these (and more) in Latvia. I've myself fixed a lot of the obvious ones before. Many of the ones remaining are harder-to-fix cases.
