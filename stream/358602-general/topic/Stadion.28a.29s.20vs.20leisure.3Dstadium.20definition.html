Topic: Stadion(a)s vs leisure=stadium definition

DodoGTA (May 15 2026 at 15:50):

Hello braliukai (again)

Is there a proper OSM definition of "stadions" in Latvia? Because in Lithuania, "stadionas" can (informally) mean any sports facility with a running track; regardless whether there's proper seating facilities or a clear boundary (which leads to leisure=stadium areas with a running track as the outline or multiple sport facilities being grouped as that instead of (likely) leisure=sports_centre)

The reason why I'm bringing this up is because there's a lot of leisure=stadium objects without a name in Latvia/Lithuania that are likely to have the incorrect tagging which should be cleaned up (also because a mapper responded to my concerns in a changeset that adds another leisure=stadium area with not a good answer)

HellMap (May 15 2026 at 16:08):

Hello, I don't think there has ever been any formal discussion on this. In practice a lot of leisure=stadium are tagged on school "stadiums" by local mappers. I guess it's because "stadions" is how it's also referred to in Latvian (which is one of those things like "meadow" that leads to mistagging). I guess looking at it, it's incorrect. It should most likely be leisure=sports_centre in many cases. Pretty much always for schools. So I guess I agree with you. Looking at overpass, there is a lot to fix in Latvia too.
