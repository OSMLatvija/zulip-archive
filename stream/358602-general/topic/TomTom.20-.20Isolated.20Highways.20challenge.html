Hi Latvian community!
My team at TomTom created a MapRoulette challenge you may find useful – Isolated Highways - https://maproulette.org/browse/challenges/39508. We identified 129 elements which are disconnected by analyzing OpenStreetMap highway network for the whole country.

There is information on the GitHub page - https://github.com/tomtom-international/open-data/issues/59 - and challenge description that may help you resolve the tasks, but please do not hesitate to contact me with any inquiries, remarks or comments.

Our editors may also work on some of the tasks in a couple of weeks, using the detailed instructions in the MapRoulette challenge.

Regards,
Tomasz
