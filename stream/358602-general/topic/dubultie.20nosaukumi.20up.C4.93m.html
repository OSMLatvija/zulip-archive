i-ky (Jan 18 2023 at 15:01):

OSM Wiki saka:

If the linear way is tagged with name=*, do not also tag the area with the same name, following the One feature, one OSM element principle.

Latvijā diezgan daudz tādu upju ar name:

[out:json][timeout:25];
{{geocodeArea:Latvia}}->.searchArea;
(
  area[natural=water][water=river][name](area.searchArea);
  relation[type=multipolygon][natural=water][water=river][name](area.searchArea);
);
out geom;

:danger: Vaicājums nepārbauda, vai ir ar name, to jāpārbauda pirms ņemt name nost no .
