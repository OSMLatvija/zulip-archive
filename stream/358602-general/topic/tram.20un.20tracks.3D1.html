Mums visas tramvaja sliedes ir uzzīmētas katrais sliedei un ortofoto ļauj izšķirtspēju nezīmēt aptuveni. Daudzām sliedēm ir norādīts tracks=1. (Dažām bija nepareizi tracks=2- izlabots). Šī ir default vērtība.

No wiki: "Number of parallel tracks in close proximity when mapped as one single way representing all tracks. If not given means unknown and defaults to 1. In many parts of the world the tracks are being drawn out separately so that there is more detail, in which case this tag isn't used." (https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:railway#Tracks)
