i-ky (Jan 30 2023 at 16:31):

Izskatās, ka Polijā ir līdzīgi noteikumi. Viņi liek dismount default'ā ar piebildi:

Note that cyclists can be allowed on footways in some cases

  • where footway is wider than 2m and next to road allowing more than 50 km/h (taggable with bicycle=yes so not a problem)
  • footways which are not sidewalks (not entirely clear legal situation!)
  • during very bad weather conditions - but it is still not allowed if explicitly signed. So highway=footway footway=sidewalk without bicycle=* will not allow in normal situation but will allow cyclists during bad weather conditions, while highway=footway footway=sidewalk bicycle=no will not allow this

...un laikam tomēr pievieno daudz bicycle=yes tagu.
