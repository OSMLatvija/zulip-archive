Šeit ir aprakstīta līdzīga problēma ar Mapillary+Discourse.
Cik es sapratu, Zulip saprot oEmbed un Open Graph tagus.
Mapillary itkā atgriež <meta name="og:image" ...>, bet dažreiz tur ir vienkārši content="https://www.mapillaty.com/assets/dotcompass.png".
Tāpēc problēma var būt Mapillary laukā...

Image preview of mapillary link
in a topic, linking a mapillary image gives link, not the image. for example, the topic How to tag an arch **symbolising** entry to area holds links to images on different platforms, only the ones on mapillary do not get rendered as images.
Open Graph protocol
The Open Graph protocol enables any web page to become a rich object in a social graph.
