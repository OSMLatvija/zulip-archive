Ar šo query var atrast visus highway, kas sakrīt ar railway=tram Rīgā:

[out:json][timeout:300];
{{geocodeArea:Riga}}->.search;
way[railway=tram](area.search)->.trams;
foreach.trams->.tram(
  way[highway][area!=yes](around.tram:0)->.highways;
  foreach.highways->.highway(
    node(w.tram)(w.highway);
    if (count(nodes)>1) {
      .highway out geom;
    }
  );
);

Atrod arī highway, kas vienkārši vismaz divas reizes krusto railway=tram, bet tur vajag jau ar acīm skatīties. :)
