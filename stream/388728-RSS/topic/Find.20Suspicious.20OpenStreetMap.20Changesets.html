Yeah, once you look at how official address data is assigned, it's not even for a building as such, but rather for that specific point/coordinate in that plot/property/location. Latvia just happens to have open and fairly accurate address data that can be imported to OSM. Noramlly, you would need to survey addresses from like street signs and house number plaques, so such "empty" addresses don't get added in many places. But technically there doesn't have to be a building or even anything else there - it's still the address of the location, like for legal or delivery purposes.
